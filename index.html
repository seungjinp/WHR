Graph 2: Relationships between the factors used to determine the overall rankings

This scatter plot explores some of the relationships between the factors that were used to calculate the overall happiness score. Corruption, Social Support, Generosity, and Freedom to make Life Choices are the national average of binary (0 or 1) responses to the questions noted below. Each point is colored based on its regions, and the size of the circles are determined by population sizes.
